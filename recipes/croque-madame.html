Croque Madame

Description

This dish is a verynutritiousmeal that will fulfill your daily macros and help you achieve your goals with a satisfying taste!

This dish takes a little bit of time to make but iswell worth it. It takes a total time of 40 minutes with a 15 minute prep time and 25 minute cook time, wit a total of 2 servings!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons salted butter, softened, divided
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill, or to taste (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 thin slices Black Forest ham
  • 4 slices white bread, lightly toasted
  • 2 ounces shredded Gruyère cheese
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
